Our Story

Where every thread is woven with intention and love

✦ Est. 2021 · Nairobi, Kenya

Born from a mother's impossible search

Terry Tender Wear started with a simple, urgent question: "Why can't I find baby clothing in Kenya that is both beautiful and truly gentle on my daughter's skin?"

After months of buying itchy, poorly-made local clothing and expensive imported pieces that didn't suit the Kenyan climate, founder Terry decided to build the solution herself. She spent a year sourcing hypoallergenic fabrics, studying baby garment construction, and testing every piece on her own daughter before selling a single item.

Today, Terry Tender Wear dresses babies across all 47 counties of Kenya. We remain a small, personal business — and we plan to keep it that way. Every order is personally reviewed. Every customer is known by name.

✦ From the Founder

A personal note from Terry

"Every morning I remind myself: somewhere in Kenya, a baby is wearing one of our pieces today. That thought keeps every standard impossibly high."

Building Terry Tender Wear has been the greatest challenge and the greatest joy of my life. I started this not to build a business, but to solve a problem I felt personally — and I quickly discovered thousands of other Kenyan mothers felt the same way.

We are small on purpose. I want to know every customer. I want to reply personally on WhatsApp. I want to know the name of your baby. That's the kind of business I'm proud to run.

Thank you for trusting us with your most precious person.

— Terry, Founder & CEO
